.. title:: MULTIBOOT2

.. option:: CONFIG_MULTIBOOT2

*Multiboot2 support*

Type: ``bool``

Help
====

Support boot ACRN from multiboot2 protocol. Multiboot2 support is needed for
some EFI platforms to get correct ACPI RSDP, it also could provide host efi
information for hypervisor.

===================
